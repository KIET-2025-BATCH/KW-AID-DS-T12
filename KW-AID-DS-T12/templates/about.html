About The Project

Understanding our Product Category Classification System

Bag of Words

Our system uses the Bag of Words technique to transform product descriptions into numerical features. This representation captures the frequency of words in the text without considering word order.

Machine Learning

We employ a Logistic Regression classifier, which works well with text data. The model learns patterns from thousands of pre-classified product descriptions.

Multiple Categories

Our system can classify products into various categories like Fruits&vegetables, Clothing, Home & Kitchen, Beauty, Household,Gourmet & World Food , and Bakery, Cakes & Dairy.

Performance

The model delivers fast and accurate predictions with a vocabulary of over 5,000 terms. It processes product descriptions in real-time with confidence scores to indicate prediction reliability.

Technical Details
Vectorization

We use CountVectorizer from scikit-learn to transform text data into a matrix of token counts. This process:

  • Tokenizes the text (splits into words)
  • Builds a vocabulary of known words
  • Encodes documents as vectors
Classification

Our Logistic Regression model:

  • Calculates word weights per category
  • Uses sigmoid function for probability estimation
  • Optimizes parameters using maximum likelihood estimation
